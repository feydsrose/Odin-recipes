Sweet Roll

Who doesn't like a sweet roll?

The skyrim inspired recipe takes ~45 mins to make

Sweet Roll
  • 1 C unsalted butter
  • 1 2/3 C granulated sugar
  • 2 C flour
  • 5 eggs
  • 1/2 Tsp salt
  • 1 Tsp baking powder
  • 2 Tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 C heavy cream (sub whole milk)
The glaze
  • 4 Tbsp unsalted butter
  • 2 Tbsp milk
  • 2 C powdered sugar
  • 1 Tsp vanilla extract
  • *optional use 1 tbsp cream cheese for thicker glaze

Directions

Roll directions
  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Put the sugar and butter in a bowl and mix them together
  3. Mix for 5 mins until mixture becomes paler and fluffy
  4. Add the eggs one at a time while mixing until all is combined
  5. Add the heavy cream and mix well
  6. Add the flour, vanilla, baking powder, and salt
  7. Grease the mini bundt pan and spoon in the batter
  8. Bake for 20 mins or golden brown
  9. rest for 5 mins and transfer to wire rack
Glaze directions
  1. Place butter in a bowl and melt it in the microwave
  2. Add milk and mix
  3. Add powdered sugar and vanilla and mix until smooth
  4. Pour over the cooled sweet rolls
